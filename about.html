Welcome to my World

Hi, I am Susan.

I am happy and excited that you are here.
I am a piano teacher and early childhood music educator living in Toronto.

In my work as a teacher I am very fortunate to be working with my students right from the early stages of their lives when they are babies and toddlers and with many of them, all the way through to adulthood. I hope to help bring out the very best in each student.

In our pursue of knowledge, skills and artistry, I hope that our learning journey together will nourish our spirit, our mind and body.

As an aspiring website developer, I seek to create websites that are effective, efficient, aesthetically pleasing and reflective of the characteristic and message in each brand and company.

I fancy all things DIY and upcycling and I am a huge fan of organic farming and sustainable practices.
To cultivate my spiritual aspect, I work on deepening my gratitude towards God, nature, materials, people and matters through my work and daily interactions with people. I look forward to working with as many people as possible to expand the circle of gratitude and happiness.
